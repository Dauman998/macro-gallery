## 🖥️ Your Offline Workflow

### The Beautiful Edit-Save-See Loop:

**1. EDIT** (in TextEdit or VS Code)
- Open `index.html` (or any file)
- Make changes to your text
- Change your name, photos, whatever!

**2. SAVE** (Cmd+S)
- Just save the file
- That's it!

**3. SEE** (in your browser)
- Switch to Safari/Chrome where your site is open
- Press Cmd+R (refresh)
- See your changes INSTANTLY!

**Repeat forever!** No internet needed at all! 🎨
