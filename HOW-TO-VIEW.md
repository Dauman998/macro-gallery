# 👀 How to See Your Website

## 🖥️ RIGHT NOW (On Your Computer)

You can see your website **immediately** without uploading anything!

### Method 1: Just Double-Click (Easiest!)

**Steps:**
1. Open your `macro-gallery` folder on your Mac
2. Find `index.html`
3. **Double-click it**
4. 🎉 Your website opens in your browser!

**That's it!** You're looking at your home page.

**To see other pages:**
- Click the menu links (Portfolio, About, Contact)
- Or double-click `gallery.html`, `about.html`, or `contact.html` directly

### Method 2: Right-Click and Choose Browser

**Steps:**
1. Find `index.html` in your folder
2. **Right-click** (or Control+click)
3. Choose "Open With" → Safari (or Chrome, Firefox)
4. Done!

---

## 🌍 ONLINE (So Others Can See It)

To put your site on the internet so ANYONE can visit it:

### Step 1: Upload Your Files to GitHub

**If you haven't already:**

1. **Go to GitHub:**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to: https://github.com/Dauman998/macro-gallery

2. **Upload your files:**
   - Click the "Add file" button (top right)
   - Click "Upload files"
   - **Drag these files** from your computer:
     - index.html
     - gallery.html
     - about.html
     - contact.html
     - navigation.js
     - styles.css
     - images/ folder (if you have photos)
   
3. **Save them:**
   - Scroll down
   - Click "Commit changes" button
   - Wait a few seconds

### Step 2: Turn On GitHub Pages

**One-time setup (takes 2 minutes):**

1. **Go to Settings:**
   - On your repository page (github.com/Dauman998/macro-gallery)
   - Click "Settings" (gear icon at top)

2. **Find Pages:**
   - Look at the left sidebar
   - Scroll down
   - Click "Pages"

3. **Enable it:**
   - Under "Source" you'll see a dropdown
   - Select: **main** (or master)
   - Click "Save"

4. **Wait:**
   - GitHub will show "Your site is ready"
   - Takes 1-2 minutes

5. **Get your URL:**
   - You'll see: "Your site is published at https://..."
   - That's your website address!

**Your website will be at:**
```
https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/
```

### Step 3: Share Your Website!

**After 1-2 minutes, your site is LIVE!**

Anyone can visit:
- **Home:** https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/
- **Portfolio:** https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/gallery.html
- **About:** https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/about.html
- **Contact:** https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/contact.html

## 🔄 After Making Changes

### Local Preview (On Your Computer):

1. Edit your files (change text, add photos, etc.)
2. **Save** (Cmd+S)
3. Go to your browser where site is open
4. **Refresh** (Cmd+R)
5. See your changes!

### Update Online Version:

**After you edit files locally:**

1. Go to GitHub: https://github.com/Dauman998/macro-gallery
2. Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
3. Drag your **updated files** (overwrite old ones)
4. Click "Commit changes"
5. Wait 1-2 minutes
6. Refresh your live site: https://Dauman998.github.io/macro-gallery/
7. Changes appear!

**Or use GitHub Desktop (easier for frequent updates):**
1. Download GitHub Desktop: https://desktop.github.com
2. Install it
3. Sign in with your GitHub account
4. Add your `macro-gallery` folder
5. When you make changes, it shows them
6. Click "Commit" then "Push"
7. Changes go live automatically!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I double-clicked index.html but nothing happened"
**Fix:** Right-click → Open With → Safari (or Chrome)

### "The file opened in TextEdit"
**Fix:** Right-click → Open With → Safari

### "I don't see my changes online"
**Fix:** 
1. Did you upload the updated files? (Not just save on your Mac)
2. Wait 2-3 minutes for GitHub to update
3. Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R
4. Try private/incognito window

### "GitHub Pages isn't showing up in Settings"
**Fix:**
1. Make sure you uploaded files first
2. Wait a few minutes
3. Refresh the Settings page
4. Check you're in the right repository

### "My custom domain isn't working"
**Fix:** See `HYBRID-WIX-GITHUB.md` - DNS can take 24 hours
