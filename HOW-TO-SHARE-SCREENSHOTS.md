## 📸 How to Take Screenshots on Mac

**Select Area:** Cmd + Shift + 4
- Click and drag to select area
- Screenshot saves to Desktop

**Whole Screen:** Cmd + Shift + 3
- Captures entire screen
- Screenshot saves to Desktop

**Window Only:** Cmd + Shift + 4, then Spacebar
- Click on window to capture
- Screenshot saves to Desktop
