## 📋 Simple Questions

### 1. How many lines is your name?

□ **One line** (everything together)
   - Example: DAN BAUMAN | PHOTOGRAPHY

□ **Two lines** (name on top, photography below)
   - Example:
     ```
     DAN BAUMAN
     PHOTOGRAPHY
     ```

---

### 2. How is the text capitalized?

□ ALL CAPS (DAN BAUMAN | PHOTOGRAPHY)
□ Title Case (Dan Bauman | Photography)  
□ lowercase (dan bauman | photography)
□ Mixed (DAN BAUMAN | photography)

---

### 3. What's the font style/feeling?

□ Bold and modern (thick, strong letters)
□ Thin and elegant (light, sophisticated)
□ Classic serif (traditional, timeless)
□ Script/handwriting (artistic, personal)

**Or just describe it:**
- "Professional but modern"
- "Clean and minimal"
- "Bold and impactful"

---

### 4. How is it aligned?

□ Left side
□ Centered
□ Right side

---

### 5. Any separator between name and "photography"?

□ Vertical bar: dan bauman | photography
□ Dash: dan bauman - photography
□ Slash: dan bauman / photography
□ Nothing (two separate lines or spaces)

---

### 6. Where's the hamburger menu?

□ Top-right corner
□ Top-left corner

---

### 7. Any special spacing or formatting?

Examples:
- "Extra spacing between letters"
- "Name is bigger than photography"
- "Everything same size"
- "Name in different color"
