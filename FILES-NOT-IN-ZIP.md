# 🚨 How to Get All Your Files (They're Here!)

## 😅 What Happened

When you downloaded the ZIP, GitHub gave you the **main branch** which only has the README.

All your **16 guides and 4 website pages** are on a different branch called `copilot/create-photography-portfolio`.

**Don't worry - your files exist! You just need to get them from the right place!**

---

## ✅ SOLUTION: Merge the Pull Request

All your files are waiting in a "Pull Request" (PR). You just need to merge it!

### Step-by-Step (2 Minutes):

**1. Go to your Pull Requests:**
   - Open Safari or Chrome
   - Go to: **https://github.com/Dauman998/macro-gallery/pulls**

**2. You'll see a Pull Request:**
   - It's called something like "Create photography portfolio" or "Add multi-page website"
   - **Click on it**

**3. Scroll down and click the GREEN button:**
   - It says **"Merge pull request"**
   - Click it!

**4. Confirm the merge:**
   - Click **"Confirm merge"**

**5. Done! Wait 10 seconds**

**6. Now download again:**
   - Go to: https://github.com/Dauman998/macro-gallery
   - Click green **"Code"** button
   - Click **"Download ZIP"**
   - This time you'll get EVERYTHING! ✅

## 🎓 What's a Branch?

**Think of it like drafts:**

- **Main branch** = Published version (currently empty except README)
- **Other branch** = Draft with all your new work
- **Merging** = Moving draft to published

**After you merge:**
- Main branch will have everything
- You can download from main
- Everyone can see your work
